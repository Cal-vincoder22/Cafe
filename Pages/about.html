About Café Mocha

Our story, our passion, our commitment to excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Café Mocha began as a small family-run coffee shop and has blossomed into a cherished community hub, all thanks to the passionate vision of its founder, Mr. Mpho Calvin.

His dedication to crafting the perfect cup, using only the finest ethically sourced beans, is matched only by his warm, welcoming presence. Stepping into Café Mocha is like being welcomed into Mpho's own home, where the rich aroma of expertly roasted coffee mingles with the sound of friendly conversation.

What started as a dream to share exceptional coffee with Johannesburg has grown into a genuine sanctuary that nourishes both the body and the soul.

Our Mission

At Café Mocha, our mission is deeply rooted in a commitment to excellence and connection. We meticulously source premium, ethically grown coffee beans, roasting them in-house to unlock their fullest flavor and complexity.

This dedication extends to our bakery, where we craft fresh, artisanal pastries daily, using local ingredients to create delightful companions to your beverage.

Beyond what we serve, we are devoted to cultivating an atmosphere of genuine warmth and inclusivity. We strive to be more than just a coffee shop; we aim to be a vital gathering place—a comfortable living room for our community.

Our Values

Sustainability

We implement eco-friendly practices, from compostable packaging to energy-efficient equipment and waste reduction programs.

Quality

Every ingredient is carefully selected, every recipe perfected, and every cup crafted with attention to detail and passion.

Community
