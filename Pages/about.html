Our Story

Founded in 2018, Café Mocha began as a small family-run coffee shop and has blossomed into a cherished community hub, all thanks to the passionate vision of its founder, Mr. Mpho Calvin. His dedication to crafting the perfect cup, using only the finest ethically sourced beans, is matched only by his warm, welcoming presence. Stepping into Café Mocha is like being welcomed into Mpho's own home, where the rich aroma of expertly roasted coffee mingles with the sound of friendly conversation, creating a genuine sanctuary that nourishes both the body and the soul.

Our Mission

At Café Mocha, our mission is deeply rooted in a commitment to excellence and connection. We meticulously source premium, ethically grown coffee beans, roasting them in-house to unlock their fullest flavor and complexity, ensuring every espresso shot poured and every cup brewed is nothing short of exceptional. This dedication extends to our bakery, where we craft fresh, artisanal pastries daily, using local ingredients to create delightful companions to your beverage. Beyond what we serve, we are devoted to cultivating an atmosphere of genuine warmth and inclusivity. We strive to be more than just a coffee shop; we aim to be a vital gathering place—a comfortable living room for our community where conversations flourish, ideas are born, and everyone feels a sense of belonging. From the friendly smile that greets you to the carefully curated ambiance, every detail is designed to create a welcoming space where you can relax, recharge, and feel at home.

Our Values

  • Quality ingredients sourced locally when possible
  • Sustainable practices in our operations
  • Excellent customer service
  • Supporting local artists and musicians
